Education

This page is dedicated to Toweka's Educational history and outlines the skills he gained whilst studying; skills that are either transferable or non-transferable.

Leeds Beckett University

Course: BSc (Hons) Biomedical Sciences – 2:2

  • Laboratory Work – three years experience in different types of laboratory studies including toxicology, microbiology, pharmacology and physiology whilst gaining vital experience in aseptic techniques and normal laboratory practice.
  • Dissertation/Project – my final year project was to study the effects of temperature on the fluorescence intensity of a fluorophore; which gave me experience in working with sensitive materials like fluorophores which need to be kept in specific conditions as well as essential practice using the Horiba fluoromax-4 spectrophotometer and its partnered software FluorEssence. Serial dilutions and buffer preparation were also key components of the project.
  • Analytical/Problem Solving Skills – experience in critically reviewing scientific papers for practical write ups as well as essays.
  • Computerized Statistics – experience using SPSS software for statistically analysing data using specific methods such as; Chi-squared test, Mann-Whitney test and the t-test.
  • Data Analysis/Interpretation – all practical write ups/reports compiled during my time at university required me to analyse them, whether it was simple analysis of the correlation between the two variables or advanced use of the SPSS software.
  • Project Management – management of my final year project was a huge task, as I constantly had to make progress on it whilst still keeping up with other modules. Smaller project management tasks include group work like the enterprise module in third year.
  • Organisation/Time Management – highly organised with very good time management, as at times I had university work and my job at the liquorist to deal with, as well as extra-curricular commitments.
  • Oral/written communication – Great English writing skills applied in essay writing and fluent oral communication exemplified in presentation work done.
  • Teamwork – gained from all aspects of my course from laboratory work to group presentations and extra-curricular activities.
